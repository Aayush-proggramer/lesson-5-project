Facts about Hockey

Origins The origins of hockey can be traced back to ancient Greece, Egypt, and Iran, where stick-and-ball games were played. The first rules of modern hockey were established in the United Kingdom in 1876.

Popularity Hockey is the second most played sport in the world, after football. It's played by over 3 million people in more than 100 countries.

Olympic history Ice hockey first appeared in the Olympics in 1920, and women's hockey was added in 1980. In 1995, an agreement between governing bodies and player associations allowed professional athletes to compete in the Olympics.
